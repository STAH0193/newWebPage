mitch stahlmann

Mitch Stahlmann Oakland based improvisor and composer/performer. His personal practice is dedicated to revealing the unearthed tones and uncharacteristic behaviours of the flute alongside proprietary electronic systems. His work has been presented in collaboration with and alongside Kronos Quartet, Meredith Monk and Zeena Parkins.
Notable releases: Into the Wish (Mondoj), Signal Quest (Orange Milk), RISA (self released).
BM Music Performance - University of Minnesota Twin Cities
MFA Electronic Music and Recording Media - Mills College
